SMMRY

Using the SMMRY Api, Tldr will summarize long form articles by 85 - 90%. No need to worry about the details, now you can just skim through the important parts of every article.

React Native

Text-to-Speech

With the Addition of React Native Text To Speech, users now have the option to listen to article summaries. Perfect for the gym, listen to all the articles in your feed as you work out!

Technologies

React-Native & Redux

We implemented React-Native to built a resuable frontend architecture and organized data flows with Redux.

Firebase

We used Firebase to authenticate user email and passwords and maintain their subscription preferences.

Ruby on Rails

We developed a specialized API to collect news articles and store their associated summaries to a Postgres database.
